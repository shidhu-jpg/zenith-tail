Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after confirmation via WhatsApp.

Delivery Time: Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days across India. Remote areas may take up to 10 days.

Free Shipping: All orders above ₹499 qualify for free shipping. Orders below ₹499 incur a flat shipping fee of ₹49.

Tracking: Once shipped, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp.

Note: We currently ship within India only.

↩️ Returns & Refunds

Return Window: We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the product is unused and in its original packaging.

Damaged Products: If you receive a damaged or defective product, send us a photo on WhatsApp within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund.

Refund Process: Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method or as store credit.

How to Initiate: Contact us on WhatsApp at +91 93417 84664 with your order ID and reason for return.

How We Use It: Your information is used solely to process and deliver your orders, and to send you order updates via WhatsApp.

Data Storage: Order and account data is securely stored using Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption.

We Never Sell Your Data: Your personal information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact: For any privacy concerns, reach us at support@zenithtail.in.
